### Stream 12 - Clarification: It's Not Size, It's Zoom

Important: Question marks don't change size. They're all the same physical size in world space. The camera zooms in/out (changes distance), making them **appear** larger or smaller on screen. At different zoom levels:
- **Zoomed in close**: Question marks appear large (camera is close)
- **Default view**: Question marks at comfortable viewing distance
- **Bird's eye**: Question marks appear as dots (camera far away)
- **Max zoom out**: All somethings collapse to appear as single dot (camera extremely far)

The "size" is perception based on camera distance, not actual scale of objects.
